 qUANTUM

Description


An In-school organization to inspire students to create projects and opportunities to compete in international competitions. This organization provides students with the tools necessary to create new ideas and prepare them for the future, with community always in mind. Some of the event that we host are We do & care (promoting STEM for girls), summer workshops for middle school and high school students, and public conferences to kids to hopefully inspire them to follow engineering.

My contribution

Founder and Representative

I am the founder and representative of Quantum, which started with me looking into opportunities to unite students with similar visions and motivation to do more than just attend school. Quantum has been able to grow and develop over time with student from all levels and different careers (Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science, Mechatronics Engineering, Accounting, and Digital Design). We aim to change the community of Tijuana in a way where students that aspire to follow the STEM field have opportunities to grow and follow their dreams.
