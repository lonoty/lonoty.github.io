Volunteer teacher

Description

Volunteer high school and middle school teacher for an after school program created by my organization with the aim to prepare future engineering students with tools that can be useful such as basic electrical skills, programming skills and 3D modeling and printing.  

At the end of the course the idea is for the students to use the tools that were thought and create a final project that they feel motivated to do. This structure allows for the students to get a glimpse of what they will want to do in a near future.
