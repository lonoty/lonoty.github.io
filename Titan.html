FRC Team: 2543 Titanbot

FRC team from Eastlake High school located in Chula Vista, CA where I was member from 4 years. I was Head of the electrical department making all the wiring diagrams and planning the wires for a clean and easy to maintain system. Also working on the CAD design for the drive train and custom gearboxes to make sure that the robot had enough torque and speed to navigate across the playing field.
